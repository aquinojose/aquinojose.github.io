REMINDERS

Gift:

Your presence at our wedding means the world to us. Should you wish to honor us with a gift, a cash contribution or bank transfer would be greatly appreciated, as it will help us build our future together. We are truly grateful for your generosity and support as we begin this new chapter in our lives.

Plus one:

Given the limited space at our venue and the intimate nature of our celebration, we’ve made the difficult decision to invite only our immediate family and closest friends. We truly hope you understand, and we are deeply grateful for your support and kindness as we celebrate this special day with those nearest to us.

Children:

While we absolutely adore your little ones, due to limited space, we are only able to accommodate children of immediate family and close friends. We hope you understand, and we sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.

Unplugged Wedding:

We warmly invite you to sit back, relax, and enjoy the moment with us. In order to truly embrace this special occasion, we kindly ask that you refrain from using cameras or mobile phones until after the ceremony. We’ve hired professional photographers to capture every beautiful moment, and we promise to share the stunning photos with you afterward. Your understanding and presence mean so much to us as we celebrate together.

Wedding Parking Area:

We apologize for the limited parking at the wedding venue. However, you may park at Walter Mart Muñoz, which is about a 7-minute walk to the venue. If you prefer not to walk, tricycles are available to take you directly to the venue for your convenience. We appreciate your understanding.
