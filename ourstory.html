Our Journey Together

We first met through mutual friends from FEU, and little did we know, that simple connection would lead to something so much more. At the time, neither of us was expecting anything serious, but fate had other plans. What started as casual interactions turned into deep conversations, and soon, we realized there was something special between us. On December 8, 2016, we officially started our relationship, marking the beginning of a beautiful journey together.


Living together was a big step, and at times, it was challenging. There were disagreements, misunderstandings, and moments where we wondered if we could truly make it work. But through it all, we learned that love isn’t just about the easy moments—it’s also about growing together through the tough times. As we approach our 9th year in 2025, we look back at all the lessons we've learned and all the challenges we've overcome, and we’re stronger than ever.


As time went on, we discovered new things about each other—our quirks, our dreams, and even our flaws. Yet, instead of driving us apart, these things brought us closer. We realized that true intimacy is built on the understanding and acceptance of each other's imperfections. With each challenge, we grew stronger as a couple, and the bond we shared deepened. Through these years, our connection has only grown, and we’ve come to cherish each other more with each passing day.


It wasn’t always smooth sailing, but those moments of difficulty were the times when we found ourselves relying on each other the most. We learned to communicate better, to listen more intently, and to compromise when needed. The trust we built along the way became the foundation of our relationship, and with it, we learned that love isn’t just about finding the right person—it’s about becoming the right partner for each other. Looking back after 9 years, we know we’ve built something truly special, and we’re excited for whatever the future holds for us.
